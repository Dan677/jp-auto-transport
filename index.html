How to Prevent Damage While Transporting a Car

When you hand your car over to a transport company, your biggest concern is avoiding damage during the journey. While risks like weather, loose debris, or poor handling can exist, they are significantly reduced by working with a professional auto transport team. At JP Auto Transport, we use modern equipment and secure fastening systems to keep your car stable and safe. We also carefully inspect every vehicle before and after delivery to ensure full transparency. By following simple steps such as removing personal belongings and checking fluid levels, you can also help protect your car. A reliable transport service is not just about moving your vehicle, but about delivering it in the same condition you left it.

Seasonal Car Shipping: Moving Your Vehicle Safely in Winter and Summer

Shipping a car during extreme weather seasons requires extra planning and care. Winter brings snow, ice, and road salt, all of which can pose risks to your vehicle during transit. In contrast, summer shipping often involves high temperatures and heavy traffic, which can affect delivery times. At JP Auto Transport, we adapt to seasonal challenges by using experienced drivers and tailored transport solutions. Enclosed trailers provide protection from harsh conditions, while open carriers remain a cost-effective choice for most routes. Regardless of the season, preparation is key. Checking antifreeze levels, securing your battery, and documenting your car‚Äôs condition are important steps to ensure a smooth transport experience.

Top Tips for Preparing Your Car for Shipping

Before shipping your car, a few simple steps can make the entire process smoother and safer. Start by cleaning your vehicle inside and out so you can easily document its condition with photos. Remove all personal items, as they are not covered by transport insurance and can shift during transit. Check tire pressure, battery charge, and fluid levels to avoid leaks or mechanical issues on the trailer. Fold in mirrors and remove any custom accessories that could break off. These preparations not only save time but also reduce the risk of unexpected problems. A well-prepared car helps ensure a stress-free delivery and a better overall transport experience.

FAQ

Have questions? We have answers. Find the information you need below.

How is the transport cost calculated?
The cost depends on distanceand vehicle type. Use our free calculator for an instant estimate!
Is my vehicle insured during transport?
Absolutely! Your vehicle is fully insured against any damage from the moment we pick it up until it's delivered.
What's the cost difference between SUVs and Pickup Trucks?
SUVs usually cost less to transport compared to pickup trucks. This is because pickups are larger, heavier, and often require more space on the carrier, which increases the overall rate.
How long will it take to deliver my car?
Delivery times vary by route. Coast-to-coast trips usually take 7-10 days, while shorter distances can be completed in 2-4 days.
Do I need to be present for pickup and delivery?
You or a designated person must be present to sign the inspection report (Bill of Lading) and hand over/receive the keys.
How can I pay for the service?
We accept major credit cards and bank transfers. A small deposit is required upon booking, with the balance due on delivery.
